Why Are We Doing This?

This is more than a form—it's the foundation for our final-year project. We're conducting a professional, research-driven study to understand the real-world problems students face and turn those insights into tools that make a difference.

Our Process

Phase 1: Collect

Gathering raw, honest insights about daily student challenges through your submissions.

Phase 2: Analyze

Using NLP and data visualization to identify key themes and prioritize pain points.

Phase 3: Validate

Converting the most pressing problems into concrete, validated project concepts.

Phase 4: Build

Developing AI-powered prototypes that are inclusive, intuitive, and impactful.

How Your Input Is Used

  • Apply Natural Language Processing (NLP) to discover student pain points from text.
  • Visualize and prioritize common themes with tools like Tableau.
  • Convert validated problems into tangible, data-driven project ideas.
  • Build inclusive, user-centric prototypes powered by modern AI.

Who Can Participate?

  • Students from any year or department—your perspective is valuable.
  • Anyone with overlooked problems or novel ideas, big or small.

Your responses are strictly confidential and will be used exclusively for this academic research project.
